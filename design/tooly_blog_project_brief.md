# Project Brief: Tooly Blog (Finance Insights)

## Overview
Tooly Blog is a content-driven extension of the Tooly personal finance platform (tooly.deluxo.co.kr). It aims to provide high-quality, SEO-optimized, and AI-friendly editorial content focused on Korean finance topics, including calculator tutorials, financial explainers, and data-driven insights.

## Core Objectives
- **Content-First Experience:** Prioritize readability and scannability for long-form financial articles.
- **AI & SEO Optimization:** Structure content (TL;DRs, FAQs, structured tables) to be easily cited by AI engines and rank well in search.
- **Brand Consistency:** Maintain a clean, editorial aesthetic that aligns with the "Editorial Finance" design system.
- **Conversion focused:** Seamlessly integrate CTAs back to the core Tooly financial calculators.

## Key Screens & Features

### 1. Blog List Page
*   **Layout:** Single-column (720px max-width), mobile-first responsive design.
*   **Discovery:** Horizontal scrolling category chips (전체, 가이드, 상식, 분석).
*   **Content Cards:** Editorial-style cards with category tags, bold H2 titles, excerpts, and metadata (date, read time, author).
*   **Navigation:** Minimal header with global links and a persistent mobile bottom navigation bar.

### 2. Blog Article Page (SEO/GEO Optimized)
*   **Hierarchy:** Breadcrumbs → Category → H1 Title → Detailed Metadata.
*   **Structured Data Points:**
    *   **TL;DR Box:** Summary bullets at the start for quick consumption and AI citation.
    *   **Collapsible TOC:** Sticky navigation for long-form content.
    *   **Callout Variants:** Tip (Blue), Warning (Yellow), Info (Gray), Important (Indigo).
    *   **Data Tables:** Zebra-striped tables for financial comparisons.
    *   **FAQ Accordion:** Structured Q&A at the end of articles.
*   **Engagement:** Author bios, related post grids, and social share buttons.

## Design Principles
- **Typography:** Pretendard (18px body, 1.75 line-height) for optimal reading comfort.
- **Palette:** Soft off-white background (#fafafa), pure black text (#000000), and Indigo (#4f46e5) as the primary accent color.
- **Whitespace:** Generous spacing to reduce cognitive load.
- **Dark Mode:** Warm dark background (#0f0f10) to prevent eye strain.

## Target Audience
Korean individuals seeking clear, actionable, and data-backed personal finance advice and tool tutorials.
